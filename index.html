Nothing Hill premier health club. Where time is well spent

a unique package of high-end gym, group exercise classes, swimming, spa, tennis and squash facilities, coupled with a strong social element centred around our Club Lounge. Members can choose to exercise hard or relax and socialise with friends, all in a first class environment. We are committed to offering members the very best health and racquets club experience. Therefore we operate a cap on our membership numbers to ensure that the Club does not feel too busy and that members are able to access facilities even at peak times. Our Club is currently full, and we are operating a waiting list. We still welcome enquiries from new members and will still be running tours however there may be a delay before you can join. You will be advised upon the wait time at the tour or point of enquiry.
